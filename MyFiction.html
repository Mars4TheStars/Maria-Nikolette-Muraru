Proposal for the Gem of Radimira (Final Portfolio Project)

Description:

Includes the game concept, one lore entry, bark sheet, one quest outline, a narrative mechanic, and another sample script (the book is a button)

Game concept:

In this game, you, Radimira, are in a party of 3 that includes 2 NPCs, Koshei the Immortal and Baba Yaga. The story is not set in a world we know, but the Slavic pagan religion is the same as in our reality (though ours was heavily repressed, so the author is taking creative liberties). Yaga and Koshei have known each other for longer than they have known you, a human who has gained magic after Rojenica, a deity of fate, intervened in your death. By crossing into the territory of Veles, the god of Nav (underworld) and a being associated with magical knowledge, sorcery, and the mystical realm, Rojenica butchered your resurrection and essentially created a spirit/child of Veles. Being driven out of your village due to fear, Koshei and Yaga took you in after Yaga found you wandering in a forest. You are now aimlessly travelling the world together, having adventures. After 10 years of those travels, they stumble upon the knowledge of a magic gem that can grant wishes. All of the three characters have something they wish for, so they keep travelling, trying to find it.
