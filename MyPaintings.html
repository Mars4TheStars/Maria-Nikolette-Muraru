My Paintings

Year 2023

Fungus malus / Evil fungi

The idea behind the title is to make it seem like a real and living mushroom species. One day I read an article that talked about psychedelic mushrooms. That night, I dreamed about this weird mushroom that is a combination of carnivorous plants and hallucination-inducing mushrooms. Practically jumping out of bed, I sketched to remember the composition. The idea is simple: the mushroom is evil in ‘poisoning’ the land around it (the gradient from a poisonous green to a warmer green). The small eyes on the mushroom cap are there to creep you out. The centered eye (red) is the one that hypnotizes the prey. The tongue is made to look flat, like a road/path. Yet, the visual heaviness that is there because it’s hanging off the logs in the water makes the viewer understands that it is a tongue (plus it’s coming out of the mushroom's mouth); the moment the prey steps on the tongue, it curls up and snaps the prey into its mouth (shown in the sketch).
